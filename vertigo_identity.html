VERTIGO

Vertigo is a hypothetical upscaled rooftop bar/lounge and a pun. For it's identity, I decided to go back to reinvision Bauer's Bodoni, and its extreme thicks and thins. The original specimen was drawn by Heinrich Jost. In approaching this revival project, I also wanted to challenge myself and push my own limits.
