LETTER AS IMAGE

This was an experiment in which I took letterforms and abstracted them through deconstruction and manipulation. In order to create these compositions, it was a combination analog processes consisting of cutting, pasting, and photocopying a variety of paper substrates. Within each composition, I explored technical concepts such as symmetry, asymmetry, scale, balance, and contrast.
